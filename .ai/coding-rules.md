# Coding Rules

These rules summarize the approved engineering guidance.

- Prefer readability over cleverness.
- Follow Kotlin coding conventions.
- Keep files focused and cohesive.
- Avoid unnecessary abstractions.
- Respect module boundaries.
- Domain must not depend on UI.
- Shared business logic belongs in KMP shared modules.
- Do not duplicate financial logic.
- Do not invent APIs or business rules.
- If requirements are unclear, stop and request clarification.
- Keep commits small and reviewable.
